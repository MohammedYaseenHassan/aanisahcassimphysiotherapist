How we can help?

Experience counts!

Aanisah has completed her degree in physiotherapy in 2016 at the University of Kwa- Zulu Natal. She than continued with post graduate training in the field of Orthopaedic Manipulative therapy.

Patient- centred care

You are involved at every step! During your initial assessment we will explain your diagnosis, commence treatment, provide advice on the things that you can do and put a plan together so you know exactly what to expect.

Recognised hands-on techniques

We provide various hands-on techniques chosen carefully to suit your individual needs and deliver the best results.

Rehabilitation

Means to “restore function”. We will prescribe specific exercises to strengthen your muscles and support joints, so that you can feel stronger and confident in the activities that add meaning to your life!

Integrated-care

We understand that every person is unique and take into consideration your lifestyle, work, personality and various other factors which may be contribute to your experience or problem.

I can visit a physiotherapist when:

  • I have pain or discomfort in my body.

  • I want to move better.

  • I want to start exercising but don’t know where to begin.

  • I am exercising but I keep getting injured.

  • I have had surgery or been injured and not moving easily as before.

  • I have a chest problem and need help to clear my airways of mucus.
